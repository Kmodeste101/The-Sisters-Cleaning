About Us

The Sister's Cleaning Company is dedicated to delivering eco-friendly and efficient cleaning services throughout Winnipeg. Our mission is to provide exceptional cleaning services that promote a healthier and more sustainable living and working environment. Committed to sustainability, we offer a wide range of services, including Eco-Friendly House Cleaning, Post Construction Cleaning, Airbnb Cleaning, and Building Maintenance. Our highly trained professionals use environmentally friendly products and methods to ensure thorough cleaning and sanitation in both residential and commercial spaces. Whether you're a homeowner, business owner, or Airbnb host, The Sister's Cleaning Company is unwavering in its commitment to delivering top-notch cleaning services that are safe for you, your loved ones, and the environment. Reach out to us today for a spotless, invigorating living or working space!
